About me

I'm a Front-end Web Developer who writes clean and dry code to build aesthetically beautiful and accessible web applications. As a former entrepreneur and effective people person, I understand the importance of building products that are user-friendly and serve a purpose.

I thrive in environments where I could jump right in to solve challenges, take ownership of tasks, continuously learn and grow within a team. I have done Masters in Business Administration (Finance & Marketing) from Punjabi University, India & Bachelor of Science in Biotechnology from Guru Nanak Dev University, India along with a Certificate of Web Development Immersive from Juno College (formerly HackerYou), Toronto Canada.
